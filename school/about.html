About Us

Project Appointment MANAGEMENT System (P.A.M.S) is a type of appointment scheduling software targeted towards higher institution that helps students schedule appointment and the lecturers also place the times they will be available to meet their students. The software is delivered to Institutions over the Internet using the software as a service model.

PAMS is an efficient management system that offer students fair oppurtuinies in seeking audience with their lecturers or supervisors. with PAMS, students can make appointments with their lecturers at certain times and have the freedom to make changes as they please. No longer will students loiter the halls in varying attemps to meet their supervisors. PAMS also ensures accountability on the part of the lecturers/supervisors.

P.A.M.S software applications are browser-based systems and provide scalability and flexibility, eliminate up-front, high-cap expenditures and require no dedicated IT support or ongoing maintenance. Its open architecture allows for deployment speed, and ease of use.
